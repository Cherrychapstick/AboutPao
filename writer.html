Timothy The Kitten & A Feathered Friend

Timothy is a nice young kitten. He drinks milk and eats Meow Mix. Timothy the Kitten enjoys to look out the window and watch the passing cars. Once in a while Timothy the Kitten will occasionally meow at passing birds that he see's fly by.

One day, Timothy the Kitten meowed at a certain bird with orange feathers. The bird sat in the nearest branch to observe the curious movements of the feline. The bird was very intregued. He stopped by everyday at around the same time to say hello to Timothy the Kitten who would surely be enjoying the morning sun on the other side of the winow sill.

Their friendship was one that was strong than steal. As time progressed they celebrated the birthdays and even the Holidays together. Timothy always found a way to sneak a treat to his good ol' feathered friend.
